About Me

I am an academic researcher specializing in political science and migration research. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute. My work focuses on migrants’ political incorporation in connection with political parties, state policies, and political behavior across Europe and global contexts, using both quantitative and qualitative methods. On this site you can find an overview of my research, information about my teaching, and how to get in touch.
